PachkhanTimeWhat it means
Navkarsi = sunrise + 48 minutes. Porsi = sunrise + ¼ of the day's length; Sadh-Porsi = +⅜; Purimaddh = +½; Avaddh = +¾. Chauvihar begins at sunset. Times are astronomical estimates — confirm with your local sangh's panchang for vrat purposes.
